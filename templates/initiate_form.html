The Self Esteem Test

Refer to the-90-day-self-esteem-test For the original concept.

Self-Awareness: Shadow Revealing

Identify traits, emotions, desires, and beliefs you consider negative or limiting.

Examples: Sad, Angry, Unworthy, Fearful, Ashamed, Stuck, Inadequate, Anxious, Jealous, Unlovable


Self-Acceptance: Coping Mechanisms

Recognize and acknowledge your coping mechanisms and distractions.

Examples: Overeating, Procrastinating, Avoidance, Social media, Substance use, Overworking, Lashing out, Self-isolation


Self-Responsibility: Gratitude and Progress

List things you're grateful for and areas where you've made progress.

Examples: Supportive friends, Health, Learning new skills, Overcoming challenges, Taking initiative, Practicing self-care


Self-Assertiveness: Manifesting Confidence

Describe the qualities of your most confident, empowered self.

Examples: Confident, Strong, Kind, Assertive, Authentic, Radiant, Grounded, Creative, Inspiring
